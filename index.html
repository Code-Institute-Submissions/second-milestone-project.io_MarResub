Romania is a Lovely Place

I love Romania, the citizens are so welcoming, Bran Castle is amazing. The people are friendly and there are a lot of beautiful parks.

Great Tourist Attractions

Tourism attraction plays a dominate role in Romania, that is why the capital Bucarest has a lot of tourists attractions.

I Enjoyed The Tourist Attractions

I really enjoyed the tourist attractions. Their food is yummy, I had some Sarmale (Cabbage Rolls), Mămăligă (Polenta) and Mici (Grilled Minced Meat Rolls) it is so yummy.

Great Recipe

Woah! good food! The Romanian lamb, Drob de miel (Lamb Haggis) and their Cozonac (Sweet Bread) ... taste so good.

I enjoyed the sightseeing

I enjoyed the sightseeing, I had a lovely tour of the place of Parliament, the museum, the athenaeum and its emblematic building as well as the black sea.

I admire the history

I admire the history, their ways of life as well as their culture with delicious meals. I will re-visit next summer.
